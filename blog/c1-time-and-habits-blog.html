Time and Habits

I know I can make it work though, and need to make sure that I'm spending my time efficiently. I also need to make sure I'm sparing enough time to play around and think about things - not just complete tasks as quickly as possible.

Which timebox and habits techniques did you try?

I already do many of them. For example I always plan my week ahead on a Friday. Then re-adjust each day in the morning. I use a "north star metric" to guide my prioritisation.
