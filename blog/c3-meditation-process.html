How did the 'process over product' concept affect the way you tackled the site redesign and rebuild?

As I've gotten older I've gotten less willing to experiment with technical stuff that I'm not sure will work. As a kid I wouldn't have minded clicking a button without understanding the consequences. But now I'm more circumspect. So I find the idea of process over product to be a good reminder that experimenting is the fastest way to learn. And that we're not here to complete tasks as quickly as possible.
